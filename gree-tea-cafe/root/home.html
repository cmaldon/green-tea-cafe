Our Story

Green Tea originated in Petaluma, California in the heart of a tea loving native. In the early 2000's the owner and creator of Green Tea Cafe struggled to find good, quality tea, and it was nearly impossible to find it in a sit down cafe. There were coffee shops on every corner, but a tea shop was very hard to find, and so, Green Tea Cafe was born.

It began with finding and supplying the best loose leaf tea products from around the world, in her garage, the owner crafted tea combinations for friends and family. Soon after, a website was launched and the business took off. Green Tea Cafe has grown into a large storefront brick and mortar cafe and bistro. All the while, the company still ships the best teas to customers all over the world, sold here in our online shop.

Our Cafe

At Green Tea Cafe you can always expect to find the most delicious drinks, food, and pastries, all made with the highest quality teas from all over the world. We guarantee you will love your tea product or your money back. Come and visit Green Tea Cafe and enjoy an experience like none other.

The Tea Wall, where you can select from hundreds of delicious, fresh picked loose tea leaves to be purchased by the pound, is restocked daily, so we always have what you're looking for.

Our Tea Bar is fully stocked and a Teamaster can make you a hot or cold drink from our menu, or assist you in creating your very own custom drink.

The Bakery prepares hot and cold foods and pastries, each dish is crafted by hand in house, with our secret ingredient, green matcha tea.
